Notes and Tips

  • This dictionary's entries are in alphabetical order. (ì and ä are at the end)
  • Note that MANY more rhymes are possible with using suffixes and enclitic adpositions.
  • Use the Find function with Ctrl+F or ⌘+F to search the page.
  • It is most useful to search for some specific heading such as em: or u:
  • If you are searching for the rhymes of a specific word, search the word with a comma attached after it.
